Microsoft 365 Copilot Prompt-A-Thon

Empowering the Public Sector with AI Innovation

AI Innovation Government Sector Microsoft Partnership
February 24, 2025 6 min read
By Enfrasys Editorial Team

On February 24, 2025, Enfrasys and Microsoft Malaysia successfully hosted the Microsoft 365 Prompt-A-Thon at INTAN, a groundbreaking event that introduced civil service leaders to the transformative power of AI tools like Microsoft Copilot and the fundamentals of prompt engineering.

Event Highlights

  • AI tool mastery training
  • Prompt engineering fundamentals
  • Government workforce development
  • Microsoft x Petronas case study
  • 50% operational cost savings demonstrated
  • Future-ready skills development

The Prompt-A-Thon represents more than just training—it's a catalyst for transforming how Malaysia's public sector approaches digital innovation and AI adoption.
