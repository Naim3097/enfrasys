Event Description

Following Microsoft's historic US$2.2 billion investment in Malaysia, Enfrasys hosted a pivotal webinar to help organizations understand and prepare for the transformative impact of the upcoming Microsoft Data Centre launch.

What Was Covered:

  • Infrastructure strategy alignment with Malaysia's Microsoft Data Centre capabilities
  • Unlocking AI-powered cloud-first business models for enterprises
  • Strategic planning for cloud adoption and digital transformation initiatives
  • Leveraging local data centre presence for enhanced performance and compliance
  • Industry-specific applications and opportunities
