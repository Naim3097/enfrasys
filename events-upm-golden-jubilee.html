UPM Golden Jubilee - 50 Years of Excellence

Celebrating legacy and innovation with Universiti Putra Malaysia

Anniversary Education Sector Partnership
Event Completed
16 Feb 2025
9:00 AM - 5:00 PM MYT (GMT+8)

Event Location

Universiti Putra Malaysia (UPM)

Event Description

Enfrasys was honored to join Universiti Putra Malaysia in celebrating its remarkable 50-year journey of academic excellence. This milestone celebration represented more than just a commemoration—it was a platform to showcase how technology can enhance and transform educational experiences for the next generation of students and researchers.

Celebration Highlights:

  • 50 years of academic achievement recognition
  • Future-ready digital solutions showcase
  • Smart campus technology demonstrations
  • Research and innovation platform presentations
  • Strategic partnership announcements
