Microsoft 365 Prompt-A-Thon

Empowering the Public Sector with AI - Training government leaders in Microsoft Copilot and prompt engineering

AI Innovation Government Sector Microsoft Partnership
Event Completed
24 Feb 2025
Full Day INTAN Collaboration

Event Location

Institut Tadbiran Awam Negara (INTAN)

Strategic partnership venue for government sector AI training and digital transformation initiatives.

Event Description

The Microsoft 365 Prompt-A-Thon was a comprehensive AI training program designed specifically for civil service leaders. This collaborative initiative between Enfrasys and Microsoft Malaysia introduced government professionals to Microsoft Copilot and the fundamentals of prompt engineering, establishing a foundation for AI adoption across public sector operations.

This training program exemplifies successful public-private partnerships in building AI capabilities that support Malaysia's digital government transformation agenda.

Expert Trainers

Max Neo - Enfrasys

Leading the training delegation with extensive experience in AI implementation for government and enterprise sectors. Microsoft AI specialist and digital transformation leader.

Azhar Abdullah - Microsoft Malaysia

Microsoft Malaysia representative bringing official product expertise and strategic insights for public sector AI adoption and Microsoft 365 solutions.

Training Curriculum

Microsoft Copilot Fundamentals

Introduction to Microsoft Copilot capabilities, interface navigation, and basic functionality for government workflows.

Prompt Engineering Mastery

Advanced techniques for crafting effective prompts that generate high-quality, relevant responses for government use cases.

Public Sector Applications

Specific use cases and applications tailored for government operations, policy development, and citizen service delivery.

Implementation Strategy

Practical guidance for rolling out AI tools across government departments and measuring success and adoption.
