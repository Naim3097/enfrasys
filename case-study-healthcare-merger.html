Implementation Timeline

Month 1

Assessment & Planning

Comprehensive assessment of existing infrastructure, data mapping, and detailed migration planning to ensure zero data loss and minimal downtime.

Month 2

Infrastructure Setup

Microsoft 365 environment configuration, security setup, and integration preparation for all healthcare facilities and administrative systems.

Month 3

Pilot Migration

Small-scale pilot migration with non-critical systems to test procedures, identify potential issues, and refine migration processes.

Month 4

Full Migration

Complete data migration across all facilities using proven processes, with 24/7 monitoring and support to ensure business continuity.

Month 5

Testing & Optimization

Comprehensive testing of all systems, performance optimization, and fine-tuning to ensure optimal user experience and system performance.

Month 6

Training & Go-Live

User training programs, change management support, and full system activation with ongoing support and monitoring.
