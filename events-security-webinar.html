Event Description

In an era of increasingly sophisticated cyber threats, securing your Microsoft 365 environment is more critical than ever. This comprehensive webinar provides actionable insights and practical strategies to strengthen your organization's security posture.

Learn essential security practices for Microsoft 365 administration and protect your organization from evolving cyber threats. This free professional development opportunity is our contribution to strengthening Malaysia's cybersecurity posture.

Practical Implementation Experience

Critical Security Topics

Identity and Access Management

Implementing zero-trust principles, multi-factor authentication strategies, and privileged access management for administrative accounts.

Advanced Threat Protection

Leveraging Microsoft Defender for Office 365, threat intelligence, and automated response capabilities to combat sophisticated attacks.

Data Loss Prevention

Configuring DLP policies, sensitivity labels, and encryption to protect sensitive information across your Microsoft 365 environment.

Security Monitoring & Response

Establishing comprehensive monitoring, incident response procedures, and continuous security improvement practices.

Key Learning Outcomes

Administrative Security

  • Configuring conditional access policies
  • Managing emergency access accounts
  • Regular access reviews and auditing

  • Customer key and encryption
  • Cross-border data protection

Compliance Management

  • Security score optimization
