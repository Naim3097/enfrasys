Who We Are

Leading digital transformation through innovative technology solutions

Enfrasys is a leading Malaysian technology partner specializing in digital transformation solutions powered by AI, cloud, modern workplace productivity, and security. With over two decades of expertise, we seamlessly integrate global innovation with deep local insights, empowering businesses across diverse industries.

As a fully Malaysian-owned company with strategic Bumiputera status within the Microsoft ecosystem, we hold exclusive specializations in AI, cloud, and security technologies. Recognized as a Managed Services Provider (MSP) and Large Account Reseller (LAR), we are compliant with NACSA standards.

Our holistic hybrid/multi-cloud understanding enables us to deliver secure and value-optimized solutions across government, finance, healthcare, education, retail, industrial, energy, and telecommunications sectors, contributing meaningfully to Malaysia's digital agenda.
