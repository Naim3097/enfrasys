Event Description

Selamat Hari Raya Aidilfitri! Our annual Raya Open House is more than just a celebration—it's a reflection of the values that unite us as a community. We invite our partners, clients, team members, and their families to join us in commemorating this auspicious occasion while strengthening the bonds that make our success possible.

This celebration represents the values fundamental to our organization: unity, gratitude, forgiveness, and renewal, reflecting our commitment to inclusivity and cultural appreciation.

Celebration Program

Welcome & Traditional Greetings

Opening ceremony with traditional Malay greetings and appreciation for our guests' presence in celebrating this important cultural milestone.

Cultural Performances

Traditional music and dance performances showcasing the rich heritage of Malaysian culture and the spirit of Hari Raya.

Traditional Feast

Authentic Malay cuisine featuring traditional Raya dishes, prepared by skilled chefs and presented in a warm, communal dining experience.

Community Mingling

Open networking and socializing time, allowing guests to connect, share experiences, and strengthen relationships in a relaxed setting.

Traditional Raya Experience

Culinary Delights

  • Rendang and traditional curries
  • Ketupat and lemang
  • Traditional kuih and desserts
  • Refreshing traditional beverages

  • Cultural music and performances
  • Photo opportunities in traditional settings
  • Storytelling and cultural sharing

Family Activities

  • Interactive games for children
  • Cultural activities for all ages
  • Professional photography services

Core Values Celebrated

  • Unity - bringing people together
  • Gratitude - appreciating relationships
  • Renewal - embracing fresh opportunities
  • Community - strengthening bonds
