Digital Transformation Questions

Common questions about Microsoft technologies and digital transformation

What Microsoft technologies does Enfrasys specialize in?

As a Microsoft Gold Partner, Enfrasys specializes in a comprehensive range of Microsoft technologies:

  • Microsoft Azure: Cloud infrastructure, migration, and optimization
  • Microsoft 365: Modern workplace productivity and collaboration
  • Power Platform: Custom business applications and automation
  • Microsoft Security: Advanced threat protection and compliance
  • Data & AI: Azure AI, Machine Learning, and analytics solutions

How long does a typical digital transformation project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity:

  • Microsoft 365 Implementation: 4-12 weeks
  • Azure Cloud Migration: 3-9 months
  • Comprehensive Digital Transformation: 6-18 months
  • Custom Solution Development: 3-12 months

We provide detailed project timelines during our initial consultation phase.

What industries does Enfrasys serve?

We serve organizations across multiple industries with specialized expertise in:

  • Healthcare: Compliance, security, and patient data management
  • Education: Learning management systems and digital collaboration
  • Manufacturing: Process optimization and data analytics
  • Financial Services: Security, compliance, and digital banking
  • Government: Public sector digital transformation
  • Telecommunications: Infrastructure and connectivity solutions

What is the difference between i-Transform and i-Learning?

Both are signature products from Enfrasys designed for different organizational needs:

i-Transform
  • Comprehensive digital transformation platform
  • Enterprise-wide modernization
  • Cloud migration and AI integration
  • Cybersecurity and compliance
i-Learning
  • Learning management system
  • Educational technology platform
  • Interactive content delivery
  • Analytics and progress tracking

What data do I need for an AI project?

Data requirements depend on your specific AI application:

  • Structured Data: Databases, spreadsheets, transaction records
  • Unstructured Data: Images, text, audio, video files
  • Volume: Typically 1,000+ samples for basic models
  • Quality: Clean, labeled, and relevant to your business goals

Our team can help assess your data readiness and suggest collection strategies.
