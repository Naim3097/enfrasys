Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we protect your information and AI data.

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name, email address, phone number, company information
  • Account credentials and authentication data
  • Billing and payment information
  • Communication preferences and support requests

AI and Technical Data

  • Training data uploaded for AI model development
  • API usage logs and performance metrics
  • System configuration and integration settings
  • Error logs and diagnostic information

Usage Information

  • Website interaction data and analytics
  • AI model usage patterns and frequency
  • Feature utilization and performance data
  • Device information and IP addresses

2. How We Use Your Information

Service Delivery

  • Provide AI solutions and technical support
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Customize AI models to your specifications
  • Monitor system performance and reliability

Communication

  • Send service updates and security notifications
  • Provide technical support and troubleshooting
  • Share product updates and new features
  • Respond to inquiries and feedback

Improvement and Analytics

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve services
  • Develop new AI capabilities and features
  • Conduct research and development activities
  • Generate aggregated, non-identifying insights

3. AI Data Protection

Special Protection for AI Training Data: We implement enhanced security measures for all AI-related data.

Data Encryption

  • End-to-end encryption for all AI training data
  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • TLS 1.3 for data in transit
  • Encrypted backups and disaster recovery

Access Controls

  • Role-based access control systems
  • Multi-factor authentication requirements
  • Regular access audits and reviews
  • Principle of least privilege enforcement

Data Isolation

  • Customer data segregation and isolation
  • Dedicated AI model environments
  • Secure multi-tenant architecture
  • Data residency options available

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

Important: We never sell your personal information or AI training data to third parties.

Limited Sharing Scenarios

  • Service Providers: Trusted vendors who assist with service delivery
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or legal process
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger or acquisition
  • With Consent: When you explicitly authorize sharing

Cloud Infrastructure Partners

  • AWS, Microsoft Azure, Google Cloud Platform
  • All partners meet enterprise security standards
  • Data processing agreements in place
  • Regular security audits and compliance checks

5. Your Rights and Choices

Data Rights

  • Access: Request copies of your personal information
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Export your data in machine-readable format

AI Model Control

  • Request deletion of custom AI models
  • Control training data usage and retention
  • Opt-out of model improvement programs
  • Request model performance reports

Communication Preferences

  • Unsubscribe from marketing communications
  • Choose notification types and frequency
  • Set communication channel preferences
  • Manage consent for data processing

6. Security Measures

Technical Safeguards

  • ISO 27001 and SOC 2 Type II compliance
  • Regular penetration testing and vulnerability assessments
  • 24/7 security monitoring and incident response
  • Data backup and disaster recovery procedures

Organizational Measures

  • Employee security training and awareness programs
  • Background checks for personnel with data access
  • Incident response and breach notification procedures
  • Regular security audits and compliance reviews
